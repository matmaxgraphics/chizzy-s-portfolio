About EasyFlip

EasyFlip is a reliable and secure exchange platform that provides seamless, safe and easy trading of gift cards and bitcoin across countries. It provides users with access to sell gift cards, bitcoin and also cash withdrawal from the convenience of their location.

Project Objective

The objectives I set out to achieve in the course of this project include:

  • Create and validate design solutions
  • Comunicate clearly the design process
  • Design a user-friendly amd intuitive app
  • Discover more on giftcard and bicoin trading

The Design Process

Competitive Analysis

Similar Products (Competitors)

EasyFlip is a reliable and secure exchange platform for trading gift cards and bitcoin. In my research, I found out it has quite many competitors and I identified their marketing strategies, technologies and also the overall user experience of these competitors. Following the analysis of my research, I was able to come up with a solid marketing strategy and technology by creating and validating design solutions. I also worked on providing a simplified and easy-to-use and navigate interface, so as to yield an exciting and great user experience.

The app uses state-of-the-art procedures for all transactions, hence reflecting payments within few minutes. Also, the app is able to secure all transactions through a professionally audited exchange system with encryption.

In summary, Easyflip serves as a middleman between those who have gift cards they do not need and those who need gift cards they do not have.

User Story

The objectives I set out to achieve in the course of this project include:

  • As a user, I want to be able to use fingerprints for login, so that I can have a better account security and login faster.
  • As a user, I want to be able to access my wallet through the dashboard, so that I can easily make transactions.
  • As a user, I want to be able to check rates of each gift cards, so that I can easily see the trade profit.
  • As a user, I want to be able to see all transactions, so that I can track my trades.
  • As a user, I want to be able to sell my bitcoin using paxful wallet, so that I enjoy seamless transaction.

User Research

First, I carried out a general research on gift cards and bitcoin, and how their trading works before proceeding to the user research in order to understand the users better, their pains, frustration and goals.

Gift cards are alternative payment to cash for purchases within a particular store or related businesses. They are issued by a particular company or brand and are activated upon purchase, so they don’t need to be activated by the recipient. Gift card essentially works as a marketing tool for different brands in the customer’s pocket and also allows for partial redemption, leaving the rest of the balance stored on the gift card and eliminating cash backs.

Next, I carried out the user research by mapping out a discussion guide with a Q/A session and moderated interviews with friends and a group of persons, who are into the crypto industry and also do online trades. These groups formed my participants for the user research.

From the Interview and Q/A session conducted, I observed that;

  • About 90% of the participants interviewed falls within the range of 20 - 30 years while, the remaining percentage are above 30 years
  • 85% of the participants are male while 15% are female.
  • About 70% of the participants love to trade online because of the seamless transaction and les stress involved.
  • Over 50% of the participants pointed out that, the risks of trading on an insecure platform could be very terrible
  • Almost 90% of the participants prefer to trade bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies using paxful wallet

The Key Findings

From the user research, the key challenge of the user is deduced as follows;

  • Poor Management Of Account: The users do not wat a system that is not properly secured and managed, which could lead to their individual accounts being hacked.
  • Complexity Of The System: Some of the exchange platforms are complex. Hence, users find it difficult to easily navigate through the system.

The Solution ( EasyFlip)

  • Verification Of Users: Users will be sent a verification link to the provided email address upon sign up.
  • Low Transaction Fee: The users will be provided with the option of a paxful wallet or other wallets for their bitcoin trading.
  • Fingerprint Login: To ensure a more reliable account security, the users would have the option of authenticating fingerprint login.
  • Rate Calculator: The users can easily check the exchange rate and equivalent amount of each gift and bitcoin to be traded.
